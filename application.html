Application


Let's change how work works

LeadWise programs give changemakers practical tools to change how organizations work.

By joining our community, you will:

Become a part of our international online community of changemakers.

Be connected to companies ready to change how they work for new assignments.

Access exclusive programs including workshops, online courses, and tools to start changing organizations.

Get trained on how to host our workshops.

Gain access to periodic skill-building webinars to help improve your facilitation skills.

Get support for your business logistics and program delivery.

Learn from other changemakers and exchange best practices.

Key Benefits

Step by step program resources

Access fully-customizable resources to implement programs immediately: slides, activities, pre-work and post-work, assessment tools, handouts and so on.

International visibility

Build your profile and get access to new projects with organizations ready to change the way they work.

Streamlined logistics

Benefit from our online platform for easy and secure participant registration and payments.

Peer learning

Connect with other organizational changemakers to share experiences, real practices and get inspired.

How it Works

Step 1

Apply

  • Fill out your application and schedule a fifteen minute video interview with the LeadWise team.
  • Once you’re accepted, your profile will be displayed on our LeadWise Community website. Organizations will then be able to contact you for assignments.
  • You will also be invited to join our Slack Community for peer learning and best practice-sharing.
Step 2

SMALL GROUPS FACILITATED BY A TRAINED COACH

  • Find programs that interest you and train to implement them.
  • We’ll provide you with playbooks, webinar recordings, online platforms, and a monthly Q&A live session to answer your questions.
Step 3

STRONG COMMITMENT NEEDED

  • We will provide you with communication and sales guidelines, plus an online payment system to support you.
  • You will earn 70% of total revenue.
