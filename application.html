Application


Let's change how work works

LeadWise programs give changemakers practical tools to change how organizations work.

By joining our community, you will:

Become a part of our international online community of changemakers.

Be connected to companies ready to change how they work for new assignments.

Access exclusive programs including workshops, online courses, and tools to start changing organizations.

Get trained on how to host our workshops.

Gain access to periodic skill-building webinars to help improve your facilitation skills.

Get support for your business logistics and program delivery.

Learn from other changemakers and exchange best practices.

Key Benefits

Step by step program resources Access fully-customizable resources to implement programs immediately: slides, activities, pre-work and post-work, assessment tools, handouts and so on. International visibility Build your profile and get access to new projects with organizations ready to change the way they work. Streamlined logistics Benefit from our online platform for easy and secure participant registration and payments. Peer learning Connect with other organizational changemakers to share experiences, real practices and get inspired.

How it Works

IT’S ONLINE AND SYNCHRONOUS

This means that you’ll have set meeting times with your cohort over video chat for the weeks of the program.

SMALL GROUPS FACILITATED BY A TRAINED COACH

Over the five weeks of the program, a coach will guide your small group through the process, so you won’t be alone. Small groups will be limited to 10 people, maximum.

STRONG COMMITMENT NEEDED

In order to transform, you have to commit. Commit to attending online meetings, and commit to doing the work. Real transformation only comes through sweat and effort. You will have assignments you must work on outside of meeting times.

HIGHLY COLLABORATIVE

We use several tools, such as Slack, Zoom, and Google Apps to connect with the community and deliver the assignments. All you need is a good internet connection, a camera, and these online tools, and the will to grow to be able to participate.
